      • Sydney Opera House

      Introduction

      The Sydney Opera House is an art performing venue located in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It is designed by a Danish architect. The Syndey Opera House is a world known landmark. The opera house host more than 1500 performances per year with more than 1.2 million people attending. The house features varieties of shows including theatre, classical music, opera, cabaret, dance and more.

      Design

      It is designed in the modern expressionist design with concrete shells covering the top serving as a roof. Looking at the roof close up a chevron pattern can be seen as two colors including glossy white and matte cream.
