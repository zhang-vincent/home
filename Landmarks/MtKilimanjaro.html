Mount Kilimanjaro

Introduction

Mount Kilimanjaro is a dormant volcano in Tanzania, East Africa. It is the tallest mountain in the entire continent of Africa and it is also the highest freestanding mountain in the world. It has an altitude of about 4900 meters and is part of the Kilimanjaro National Park. This site is used mainly for mountain climbing.

Climbing

There are many climbing routes including the Arrow Glacier, the Lemosho, Machame, Marangu, the Rongai, and Umbwe. The Mweka is used only for descending. Shira is used mainly for evacuation.
