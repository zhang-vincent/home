Great Wall of China

Introduction

The Great Wall of China was built over a long period of time. It started as early as 7th century BC and the majority of the wall was built in Ming Dynasty which is 1368â€“1644. The wall is in segments are many parts are parallel in the northern section of China. The total distance 21196.18 kilometers and the construction period was 2500 years.

History

In the beginning, it was used as a fortification against provinces but later it more segments of the wall was built on the northern side of China to prevent invasions from the north.

Construction Period

    Zhou Dynasty (770-221-BC)

  1. A Qi State duke built segments to prevent invasions from other states

    2. Qin Dynasty (221-207 BC)

  2. More segments were built because the Qin Emporer wanted to secure the northern borders

    3. Han Dynasty (206-220 AD)

  3. The wall was expanded to protect the Silk Road Trade

    4. Ming Dynasty (1368-1644)

  4. Most of the wall was built and restored

    5. Qing Dynasty(1644-1911)

  5. More construction was made

    6. Modern Time

  6. In 1957 reconstruction began
