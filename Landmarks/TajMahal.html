Taj Mahal

Introduction

Taj Mahal is a structural landmark located on the south bank Yamuna river in the city of Agra. It is a mausoleum in India. The structure is made of white marble which seems changes colour over the sunlight or moonlight. It is influenced by Indian, Persian and Islamic cultures. It is 73 meters tall in height.

Origin

The massive tomb was commissioned in 1632 by a Mughal called Shah Jahan,“the King of the World,” who reigned from 1628 to 1658. The tomb was built to house his favourite wife Mumtaz Mahal, “Chosen One of the Palace.”

Cost

The construction of the tomb cost about 32 million rupees which are approximately 52.8 billion rupees in 2015 which is about $827 million American dollars.
